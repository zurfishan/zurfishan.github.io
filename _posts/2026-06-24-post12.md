# POST 12 : Learning Time Management

After going through the stressful exam week, I started thinking seriously about time management. I realized that most of the pressure I experienced was avoidable.
Delaying tasks might feel comfortable in the moment, but it creates bigger problems later. I understood that consistency is more effective than last-minute effort.
I began to plan my work more carefully. Instead of waiting for deadlines, I tried to complete tasks earlier. This small change made a noticeable difference.
Dr. Bilal Ahmad always emphasized consistency, and now I understood why. Managing time properly not only reduces stress but also improves learning.
This lesson is something I will carry forward. It will help me not just in academics, but in other areas of life as well.
