# POST 14 : Teacher’s Influence

Throughout the semester, Dr. Bilal Ahmad had a strong influence on how I approached learning. His teaching style was different from what I was used to.
Instead of focusing only on completing the syllabus, he emphasized understanding and practical thinking. This encouraged me to change my approach.
I started focusing more on concepts rather than just finishing assignments. This shift made learning more meaningful and less stressful.
His guidance helped me understand that education is not just about grades. It is about developing skills and thinking ability.
This influence will stay with me in future courses as well.
