## POST 10 — Managing Assignments Pressure

Throughout the semester, managing assignments and quizzes was not always easy. There were times when multiple deadlines came together, creating pressure.
In the beginning, I did not manage my time effectively. I often delayed tasks, thinking I would complete them later. However, when deadlines approached, everything became stressful.
Balancing Programming Fundamentals and Database Systems required consistency. Both subjects needed regular practice, and ignoring them for too long made things difficult.
Dr. Bilal Ahmad frequently advised us to stay consistent and avoid last-minute work. At that time, I did not fully follow that advice, but I understood its importance later.
This phase made me realize that time management is an essential skill. It is not just about completing tasks, but about completing them in a planned and organized way.
Learning this lesson was not easy, but it was necessary for my growth.
